Ch 1 — Basics & Tax Rates Key Points

  • Sources: IT Act 1961 + Rules 1962 + Finance Act + Circulars + SC/HC
  • AY = PY + 1 year | AY 26-27, PY = 25-26
  • 7 types of Person: I-HUF-Company-Firm-AOP/BOI-LA-AJP
  • Old Regime: 2.5L-5L-10L at 5-20-30% | New 115BAC default
  • New Regime: 4-8-12-16-20-24L at 5-10-15-20-25-30%
  • 87A Old = Rs 12,500 (NTI ≤ 5L) | 87A New = Rs 60,000 (NTI ≤ 12L)
  • Deemed income 68-69D = 78% tax (60% + 25% SC + 4%)
  • Agri income: Exempt + Partial integration for Ind/HUF/AOP
  • Tea 60-40 | Rubber 65-35 | Coffee cured 75-25 | Coffee roasted 60-40

Ch 2 — Residential Status Key Points

  • Individual: BC1 = 182+ days | BC2 = 60 days + 365/4PYs
  • R&OR: Both additional conditions (2/10 PYs + 730/7 PYs)
  • R-NOR: Either additional condition fails
  • Deemed Resident 6(1A): Income > 15L + not taxed abroad = R-NOR
  • HUF/Firm: C&M wholly outside = NR | Partly India = Resident
  • Indian Company = Always Resident
  • Foreign Company: POEM in India = Resident
  • Sec 9 Big 4: Business Connection, Salary, Dividend, Interest/Royalty/FTS
  • Gift by Resident to NR (after 5 July 2019) = Deemed Indian income

Ch 3 Salaries — Key Formulas

  • Gratuity POGA: 15/26 × Latest salary × Completed yrs (max 20L)
  • Gratuity Non-POGA: ½ × Avg 10m salary × Completed yrs (max 20L)
  • Leave salary (Non-Govt): Lowest of 4 — leave credit/10m salary/actual/25L
  • HRA: Lowest of 3 — 50/40% salary / actual HRA / rent-10% salary
  • Pension commuted: Govt = fully exempt | Non-Govt+gratuity = 1/3 | Without = 1/2
  • Standard deduction: Rs 75,000 new regime | Rs 50,000 old regime

Ch 4 PGBP — Key Rates

  • Dep rates: Building 10% | P&M 15% | Computer 40% | Intangible 25%
  • Additional dep: 20% on new P&M manufacturing | <180 days = 10%+10% next
  • 44AD: T/O up to 2Cr (3Cr digital) | 8% (6% digital)
  • 44ADA: GR up to 50L (75L digital) | 50% of GR
  • 44AE: Heavy = Rs 1000/ton/month | Other = Rs 7500/month
  • Partner rem: 6L BP = 90% or 3L (higher) | Balance = 60% | Interest max 12%
  • 43B(h): MSME — 15 days (no agreement) / 45 days (with agreement)

Ch 6 Capital Gain — Key Formulas

  • CG = FVOC − Transfer exp − COA − COI
  • LTCA: Listed equity/UTI/EOMF/ZCB = >1 yr | All others = >2 yrs
  • Depreciable assets = ALWAYS STCG (Sec 50)
  • Slump sale COA = Net worth | >3 years = LTCG
  • 54: RHP to RHP | -1+2 buy / +3 construct | 10 Cr cap
  • 54F formula: LTCG × Cost / Net Consideration
  • 54EC: Bonds 6 months | Max Rs 50L | 5yr lock
  • 112A: Equity LTCG 12.5% | 1.25L exempt | Grandfathering 31/1/2018
  • 111A: Equity STCG 20% (from 23 Jul 2024)
  • 2-6-8 km rule for urban agri land

Ch 11 TDS — Interest Formulas

  • 234A: 1% × months from due date (late return)
  • 234B: 1% × months from 1 Apr AY (short advance tax, <90%)
  • 234C: 1% × 3 months per instalment (last = 1 month)
  • 234E: Rs 200/day for delayed TDS return (max = TDS)
  • Advance tax: 15-45-75-100% on 15 Jun/Sep/Dec/Mar
  • 206AA no PAN: Higher of section rate or 20%
  • 194T (NEW 2025): Partner payments 10% if >Rs 20,000
