What is the maximum deduction allowed under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act?
👆 Tap to reveal answer
Answer
₹1,50,000 per year. Includes PPF, EPF, LIC premium, ELSS, NSC, home loan principal, tuition fees.

Trick: "PELHN" — PPF, ELSS, LIC, Home loan, NSC
