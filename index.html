🏆 Rankers Strategy

How Toppers Study

Proven strategies from CA Inter rank holders.

01
Consistency > Intensity
6 focused hours daily beats 14 scattered hours. Use Pomodoro — 25min study, 5min break. Never break the streak.
📊 Used by 90% of rank holders
02
ICAI Material First
Rank holders read ICAI SM at least 3 times. Our flashcards are extracted directly from ICAI material — use them daily.
📖 Primary source always wins
03
PYQ is Non-Negotiable
Solve last 10 years' PYQ for every subject. ICAI repeats 40–60% of topics. Pattern recognition is a superpower.
🔁 40-60% topics repeated
04
Weak Subject = Extra Time
Give 40% of study time to your weakest subject. One failing subject fails the group. Balance is survival strategy.
⚠️ Groups fail, not subjects
05
Write, Don't Just Read
CA exam is written. Practice writing full answers in ICAI format — Provision, Application, Conclusion for law topics.
✍️ Presentation = 10-15% marks
06
Amendments = Easy Marks
Finance Act 2025 amendments in Tax & GST are heavily tested. Always updated here — check the revision section.
📌 New amendment = guaranteed question
