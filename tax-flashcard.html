⚡ EXAM MEMORY TRICKS — ALL CHAPTERS

Ch 1 — Tax Rate Tricks

  • Old slab: 0-2.5-5-10L → NIL-5-20-30%
  • New slab: 0-4-8-12-16-20-24L → NIL-5-10-15-20-25-30%
  • 87A rebate: Old=₹12,500 up to 5L | New=₹60,000 up to 12L
  • Surcharge ladder: NIL-10-15-25-37% at 50L-1Cr-2Cr-5Cr
  • 115BAC = Default | 37% surcharge = GONE in new regime
  • H&EC = 4% always on (Tax + Surcharge)

Ch 2 — Residence Tricks

  • ROR = Resident + 2/10 years + 730/7 years
  • RNOR = Resident but fails any above condition
  • NR = <182 days AND not 60+365 condition
  • R&OR taxes global income | RNOR = India + controlled biz | NR = India only
  • Company: Indian = Always Resident | Foreign = POEM in India
  • Deemed Resident: Indian citizen, India income >15L, not taxed elsewhere

Ch 3 — Salary Tricks

  • HRA = Min of 3: Actual | Rent−10%sal | 50%/40%sal
  • Gratuity limit = ₹20L (all categories)
  • Leave encashment limit = ₹25L (retired)
  • Standard deduction = ₹75K (new) | ₹50K (old)
  • VRS exemption = min(Actual, ₹5L)
  • Perquisite car: ₹1800/₹2400 + ₹900 driver pm

Ch 4 — HP Tricks

  • GAV DLOP = ER always (AR irrelevant)
  • Municipal taxes: Payment basis | Owner must pay
  • Pre-construction interest: 5 equal instalments
  • Max 2 SOP from AY 2020-21
  • HP loss inter-head max ₹2L | C/F 8 years HP only
  • 115BAC: 24(b) SOP interest NOT allowed

Ch 5 — PGBP Tricks

  • Depreciable asset = ALWAYS STCG (not LTCG)
  • Half dep if asset used <180 days in 1st year
  • 44AD: 6% digital | 8% cash | T/O ≤₹3Cr
  • 44ADA: 50% GR | Profession ≤₹75L
  • 44AB audit: Biz >₹1Cr | Profession >₹50L
  • 35AD: 100% capital expenditure deduction

Ch 6 — Capital Gain Tricks

  • Holding: Listed shares/eq MF = 12M | Others = 24M/36M
  • STCG 111A = 20% | LTCG 112A = 12.5% (₹1.25L exempt)
  • 54: 1 HP | 54EC: ₹50L bonds 6 months | 54F: any asset
  • CGAS: Deposit if not invested before return due date
  • 50C: SDV used as COA if SDV > 110% of actual Prince
  • Bonus shares COA = NIL | Rights shares = Issue Prince

Ch 7 — IFOS Tricks

  • MRIDHUTLAITC = Gift exceptions (Relative, Marriage etc.)
  • Lottery/Games = 30% flat | No deduction | No exemption
  • Family pension deduction = 1/3 or ₹25,000 (lower)
  • Gift >₹50,000 without consideration = fully taxable
  • 56(2)(x): SDV > 110% of consideration = taxable
  • Dividends: 10% TDS if >₹5,000 from company

Ch 8 — Clubbing Tricks

  • Substantial interest = 20%+ shares/PSR
  • Exception: Spouse's technical qualification → no clubbing
  • Minor ₹1,500 exempt per child u/s 10(32)
  • Second generation income = NOT clubbed
  • Cross gifts = Clubbed to extent of matching amount
  • Natural love ≠ adequate consideration

Ch 9 — Set-Off Tricks

  • Intra → Inter → C/F (always this order)
  • HP + Unabsorbed dep → can C/F even if return late
  • PGBP/CG/Race horse → MUST file on time for C/F
  • Business loss ❌ Salary | HP loss max ₹2L inter-head
  • Speculative = 4yr | HP/Normal/CG = 8yr | 35AD = unlimited
  • 115BAC: HP loss cannot set-off or C/F at all

Ch 10 — Deduction Tricks

  • 80CCE = 80C+80CCC+80CCD(1) combined max ₹1.5L
  • 80CCD(1B) = Extra ₹50K NPS (over 80CCE limit)
  • 80D: ₹25K self + ₹25K parents (₹50K each if senior)
  • 115BAC: Only 80CCD(2)+80CCH(2)+80JJAA allowed
  • AMT = 18.5% ATI | Not if 115BAC or ATI ≤₹20L
  • 80G: Cash >₹2,000 → No deduction | In-kind → No deduction

Ch 11 — TDS/TCS Tricks

  • 234A: 1% pm delay in filing | 234B: 1% pm advance tax default
  • 234C: 1% pm deferment of instalment | 234F: ₹5K/₹1K fee
  • No PAN = TDS at 20% or prescribed rate (higher)
  • 15G (non-senior) / 15H (senior) = No TDS declaration
  • Advance tax: 15-45-75-100% by Jun-Sep-Dec-Mar 15
  • TDS deducted at earlier of: Payment OR Credit to a/c

Ch 12 — Return Filing Tricks

  • Company/Firm = Always file | Others = GTI > Basic exemption
  • Belated = 31st Dec of AY | Revised = same 31st Dec
  • Updated (ITR-U) = 4 years | Only increase income
  • Extra tax: 25-50-60-70% in yr 1-2-3-4
  • HP loss + Unabsorbed dep = C/F even with belated return
  • Defective = 15 days to fix or VOID (ab-initio)
