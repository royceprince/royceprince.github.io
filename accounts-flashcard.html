5 Conditions for Financial Lease
#Condition
(i)Lessee will become owner at end of lease term automatically
(ii)Lessee has option to purchase asset at price lower than fair value (bargain purchase option) and is certain to exercise it
(iii)Lease term covers major part of economic life of asset (generally 90%+ of asset life)
(iv)Asset is specialised and cannot be used by other lessees without major modifications
(v)Present value of lease rentals ≈ fair value of asset (around 90% or more of fair value)
Lease = Agreement where one party gets right to use asset against consideration or series of considerations Lessee = who gets right to use asset | Lessor = who receives consideration Finance charge method: Effective interest rate OR Rule of 78 (sum of digits)
