Ch 4–6 — AS 1, 2, 3 Tricks

  • AS 1: 2016 ke baad Depreciation Method = ESTIMATE (not Policy!)
  • AS 1: Policy change = Disclose Nature + Reason + Quantum
  • AS 2: LCNRV = Lower of Cost or NRV (item by item!)
  • AS 2: LIFO = FORBIDDEN! Only FIFO or Weighted Average
  • AS 2: RM exception = If FG below cost → RM at Replacement Price
  • AS 3: All companies except OPC → CFS mandatory
  • AS 3: Cash Equivalents = realisable within 3 months

Ch 7–9 — AS 7, 9, 10 Tricks

  • AS 7: DoC = Cost incurred ÷ (Cost + Future cost) × 100
  • AS 7: VCEI = Variations, Claims, Escalation, Incentives add to revenue
  • AS 7: Expected Loss = Recognise IMMEDIATELY (prudence!)
  • AS 9: Substantial time → PoC; Others → Completed Service
  • AS 9: I=Time; D=Declared; R=Agreement conditions
  • AS 10: Future Benefit Probable + Cost Measurable = Recognise PPE
  • AS 10: Depreciation method change = ESTIMATE (prospective only)

Ch 10–12 — AS 11, 12, 16 Tricks

  • AS 11: Monetary = Closing Rate → P&L
  • AS 11: Non-monetary = Transaction Date Rate → No remeasure
  • AS 12: Reasonable Assurance of Compliance + Receipt = Recognise
  • AS 12: NOT on cash basis! Conditions not met = Deferred Income (Liability)
  • AS 12: Revenue Grant = Match karo related expense ke same period mein
  • AS 16: QA = 12+ months; Construction + Renovation + Long-mfg stocks
  • AS 16: Start = Exp + BC + Activities; Pause = Extended interrupt; Stop = Substantially complete

Ch 13–16 — AS 17, 20, 24, FS Tricks

  • AS 17: 10-10-10 Rule: Revenue / Result / Assets ≥ 10% = Reportable
  • AS 17: 75% Rule — Reportable segments cover ≥75% of total revenue
  • AS 20: Basic EPS = (PAT – Pref Div) ÷ WA Equity Shares
  • AS 20: Bonus = Pura Saal! (Full year, no time weighting)
  • AS 20: Diluted EPS ≤ Basic EPS (always!)
  • AS 24: Disclose from date of FORMAL COMMITMENT (not just plan)
  • Schedule III: Division I = AS companies; 12 months rule for current classification

Ch 17-20 — Buyback, Amalg, Recon, Branch Tricks

  • Buyback: CRR = Paidup bought back – Fresh issue made for buyback
  • CRR only for Bonus Issue (Sec 69) | CRR Sec 55 from Revenue Reserve ONLY
  • No fresh issue 6 months; no further buyback 1 year post-buyback
  • Amalgamation: NPM if complete payment info; NAM otherwise (residual)
  • Merger: ALL assets/liabilities at book values; ≥90% ESH agree; equity only
  • Statutory reserve → Amalgamation Adjustment A/c (negative in BS)
  • Internal Recon: Capital Reduction A/c MUST close; surplus → Capital Reserve
  • IBC steps: NCLT → IRP → COC → RP → Plan → COC → NCLT → Execute
  • Branch: IFO = Fixed assets at transaction rate | NIFO = all at closing rate
  • Branch A/c (HO) = equal & opposite of H.O A/c (Branch) always

Framework & Qualitative — Tricks

  • URRC = Understandable, Relevant, Reliable, Comparable
  • A-L-E-I-E = Asset, Liability, Equity, Income, Expense (5 elements)
  • HC-CC-RV-PV = 4 measurement bases
  • GAC = Going concern, Accrual, Consistency (disclose ONLY if not followed)
  • I-ELS-CGP = 7 users of FS
  • Components = BS + P&L only; CFS = Report (not component!)
