⚡ GST Memory Tricks & Mnemonics
Key Mnemonics
🧠 GTA exemptions = DROMNA: Defence, Relief, Organic manure, Milk/salt/grains, Newspapers, Agriculture produce 🧠 Schedule III (Not Supply) = SECFAS: Services by employee, Courts, functions by Elected reps, Funeral, Actionable claims, Sale of land/completed building 🧠 ITC Utilisation = "IGST is the KING — uses all; CGST uses CGST first then IGST; SGST uses SGST first then IGST; CGST and SGST NEVER cross-utilise each other" 🧠 Blocked ITC 17(5) = FACE-BMWHT: Food/beverages, Aesthetics (beauty/cosmetic), Club membership, Employee travel, Beauty treatment, Motor vehicle (passenger), Works contract (IP), Health services, Travel (LTC) 🧠 Composite vs Mixed: "COMPOSITE = NATURALLY BUNDLED (like train ticket includes meals) → Principal supply rate | MIXED = ARTIFICIALLY BUNDLED (gift basket) → HIGHEST rate item" 🧠 Schedule I Deemed Supply (no consideration) = PAST: Permanent transfer of assets (ITC availed), Agent-Principal (invoice in agent's name), Supply between related/distinct persons, Transfer of services from overseas related entity 🧠 Export of Services (5 conditions) = SLPCS: Supplier in India, Location of recipient outside India, Place of Supply outside India, Consideration in Convertible foreign exchange, Supplier and recipient are not DDP
